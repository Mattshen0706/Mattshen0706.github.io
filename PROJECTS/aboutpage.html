WHAT IS INVICTUS A GLOBAL MOVEMENET, BRINGING FASHION TO CALISTHENICS
CALIBEARZ   x  SEISHI INTE. Project Invictus is a groundbreaking collaboration between Seishi International and Calibearz, designed to bridge the gap between calisthenics and modern pop culture through the medium of streetwear and fashion. More than just an apparel line, this project marks our first official release to the wider public, setting the stage to elevate calisthenics to new heights. At its core, Project Invictus is about more than fashion—it’s about the athletes and the unbreakable spirit they embody. The name "Invictus," derived from Greek, means "unconquerable", a testament to the resilience and strength that defines the calisthenics community. What truly makes calisthenics special to us is the global community—a collective of individuals who push beyond limits, overcome adversity, and inspire others along the way. To honor this, we’ve invited athletes from around the world to share their journeys, their struggles, and the dedication that has led them to where they are today. This philosophy is captured in the quote "Ad Astra Per Aspera"—"Through Hardships to the Stars." Project Invictus is not just a movement; it’s a testament to perseverance, passion, and the pursuit of greatness.
